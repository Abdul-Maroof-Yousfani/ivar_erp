# PF for Employee

## Overview
Employee-facing and individual tracking of Provident Fund contributions and balances.

## Actions

### 1. View PF
- **Description:** detailed view of an employee's PF balance, including employee and employer contributions.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/pf-employee/view`

### 2. Create Withdraw
- **Description:** Initiate a withdrawal from the PF account (e.g., loan against PF or final settlement).
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/pf-employee/withdraw-create`

### 3. View Withdraw
- **Description:** History of PF withdrawals.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/pf-employee/withdraw-view`

### 4. View Report
- **Description:** Comprehensive PF reports.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/pf-employee/report`

### 5. View Ledger
- **Description:** Detailed transaction ledger for PF accounts.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/pf-employee/ledger`
