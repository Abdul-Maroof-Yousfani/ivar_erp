# Final Settlement

## Overview
Calculates the final payable amount for an employee leaving the organization, including salary dues, leave encashment, PF, and deduction of any outstanding loans/assets.

## Actions

### 1. Create
- **Description:** Initiate a final settlement calculation.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/final-settlement/create`

### 2. List
- **Description:** View past settlements.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/final-settlement/list`
