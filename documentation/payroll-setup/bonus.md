# Bonus Management

## Overview
Manages the distribution of bonuses to employees, such as annual bonuses, performance incentives, or festival bonuses.

## Actions

### 1. Issue Bonus
- **Description:** Calculate and issue bonuses to eligible employees.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/bonus/issue`

### 2. View & Reports
- **Description:** History of issued bonuses.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/bonus/view`

### 3. Bank Report
- **Description:** Generate bank transfer files for bonus payments.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/bonus/bank-report`

### 4. Bonus Payslip
- **Description:** Generate and send payslips specifically for bonus payments.
- **Path:** `/hr/payroll-setup/bonus/payslip`
