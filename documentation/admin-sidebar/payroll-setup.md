# Payroll Setup

## Overview
Comprehensive module for managing employee compensation, including salary processing, allowances, deductions, and loans.

## Sections

### 1. Payroll
- **Create:** Run payroll for a specific month/year.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/payroll/create`
- **View Report:** Detailed payroll reports.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/payroll/report`
- **Bank Report:** Generate bank transfer files/reports.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/payroll/bank-report`
- **Payslips Emails:** Manage and send payslips to employees.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/payroll/payslips`

### 2. Allowance
- **Create:** Assign specific allowances to employees.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/allowance/create`
- **View:** List assigned allowances.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/allowance/view`

### 3. Deduction
- **Create:** Assign specific deductions to employees.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/deduction/create`
- **View:** List active deductions.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/deduction/view`

### 4. Advance Salary
- **Create Request:** Request for salary in advance.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/advance-salary/create`
- **View Requests:** Approval and tracking of advance salary.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/advance-salary/view`

### 5. Loan Requests
- **Create Request:** Apply for a company loan.
  - *Path:* `/hr/loan-requests/create`
- **View & Reports:** Loan status and repayment tracking.
  - *Path:* `/hr/loan-requests/view`

### 6. Increment/Decrement
- **Create:** Process salary increments or decrements.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/increment/create`
- **View:** History of salary changes.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/increment/view`

### 7. Bonus
- **Issue Bonus:** Distribute bonuses (Festive, Performance, etc.).
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/bonus/issue`
- **View & Reports:** Bonus history.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/bonus/view`

### 8. Leave Encashment
- **Create:** Process payment for unused leaves.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/leave-encashment/create`
- **List:** History of encashments.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/leave-encashment/list`

### 9. PF for Employee
- **View PF:** Employee Provident Fund balances.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/pf-employee/view`
- **Withdraw:** Manage PF withdrawals.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/pf-employee/withdraw-create`

### 10. Final Settlement
- **Create:** Calculate final dues for exiting employees.
  - *Path:* `/hr/payroll-setup/final-settlement/create`

### 11. Salary Sheet
- **Tax Certificate:** Generate tax certificates for employees.
  - *Path:* `/hr/salary-sheet/tax-certificate`
