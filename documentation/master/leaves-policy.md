# Leaves Policy Master

## Overview
Configures the rules governing leaves, such as accrual rates, carry-forward limits, and quotas per leave type.

## Actions
- **Add:** Create a new policy.
  - *Path:* `/master/leaves-policy/add`
- **View:** List policies.
  - *Path:* `/master/leaves-policy/list`
- **Manual Leaves:** Manually adjust leave balances.
  - *Path:* `/master/leaves-policy/manual-leaves`
